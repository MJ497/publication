It is a crystal clear truth that, one of the most challenged institution in this 21st century is marriage. The statistics of cases of divorce is alarming. A lot of couples are separated and this have subjected the children to an ineffective upbringing. Moreso, the issue of cheating in marriage is another bone of contention in this dispensation. But the good news is that ,in spite of all these unpleasant events, this in any way do not invalidate the honour in Marriage. Marriage is honourable. Becoming a Balanced Man

N3000

Man is a tripartite being - a spirit being that possess a soul and dwelling in a body. These three though separate,yet dependent, have to be in harmony in order to live a fruitful and fulfilled life in accordance with God's design, plan and purpose. Living a balanced life guarantees us a perfect horizontal relationship on earth while keeping unto a Divine and heavenly focus. Being balanced implies having different parts of our life effectively running while we are here on earth. Never focus on a particular area of your life at the detriment of others.
