About Olasunkanmi Salami

The Man Behind the Words

Olasunkanmi Salami was born in Lagos, Nigeria, where he developed an early passion for storytelling. His childhood was filled with books from various cultures, which shaped his unique narrative voice.

After completing his education in Literature at the University of Ibadan, he began his writing career with short stories published in literary journals across Africa. His debut novel, published in 2010, received critical acclaim and established him as a significant voice in contemporary African literature.

When not writing, Olasunkanmi conducts creative writing workshops and mentors young authors. He believes in the transformative power of stories to bridge cultural divides and foster understanding.
